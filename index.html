Who We Are

We Show Up After the Fire is Out.

The Firefighters of Southern Nevada Burn Foundation stands beside burn survivors and families struck by fire and catastrophe — from the first hours after the sirens fade, through recovery, and for every year that follows.

  • Immediate Relief

    Partnered with the American Red Cross to provide aid for the unexpected costs of catastrophe.

  • Long-Term Recovery

    Recovery equipment, support groups, counseling, burn camps and survivor conferences.

  • Prevention First

    Fire safety education that stops the burn before it ever happens.

What We Do

Four Programs. One Promise.

01

After the Fire is Out™

When the trucks pull away, the hardest part begins. We support residents through our partnership with the American Red Cross, provide immediate aid for the unexpected costs of catastrophe, and stand as a resource for every fire agency in the valley.

  • American Red Cross partnership
  • Emergency financial assistance
  • Resource hub for fire agencies
02

Burn Survivor Initiative

Healing is measured in years, not days. The BSI supplies recovery equipment, partners with local burn units to run support groups and counseling, and funds burn camps and survivor conferences where no one has to explain their scars.

  • Recovery equipment & supplies
  • Support groups & counseling
  • Burn camps & conferences
03

Save a Life

The best burn is the one that never happens. Our firefighters bring fire safety education into schools, homes and community events across Southern Nevada, building prevention awareness one family at a time.

  • Fire safety education
  • Community prevention outreach
  • Awareness campaigns
04

Fill the Fire Truck Toy Drive

Every December our trucks roll out loaded with presents. Coordinating with local businesses and nonprofits, the annual toy drive delivers gifts to thousands of local children — 30,386 smiles in the 2024 season alone.

  • Annual valley-wide toy drive
  • Business & nonprofit partners
  • Thousands of children served
